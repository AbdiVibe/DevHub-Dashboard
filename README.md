### 💾 **Data Persistence**

- ✅ All data saved locally in your browser using `localStorage`
- ✅ No account or sign-up required
- ✅ Works offline (PWA ready)
- ✅ Data persists across browser sessions
- ✅ Privacy-focused - your data never leaves your device
